## Step-by-Step

<Steps>
    <Step title="Download the App">
        Get Superwhisper for MacOS from [Our Website](https://superwhisper.com/).
    </Step>

    <Step title="Access your License Settings">
    Once installed, open the MacOS app and navigate to [Settings](https://superwhisper.mintlify.app/get-started/settings). Look for the "License" tab.

    <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/get-started/activate-mac-001.png"
    alt="Desktop Pro Activation Screen"
    />
    
    </Step>

  <Step title="License Activation">
    To complete activation, you'll need to copy the key from your iOS app. 
      1. On your iOS app, go to Settings > "Superwhisper Pro"
      2. Locate the "Reveal License Key"
      3. Tap it to reveal your key
      4. You can then type this into the "License Key" Box on the MacOS app. Be sure to keep the upper case lettering. 

    <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/get-started/activate-mac-002.png"
    alt="Desktop QR Code Activation Screen"
    />

  </Step>
</Steps>
