## Overview
This guide outlines the essential information needed to report issues effectively, ensuring quick and accurate solutions for any challenges you encounter with Superwhisper.

<Info>
For quick support, reach out at support@superwhisper.com. You can also share your issue in [Superwhisper's Discord server](https://discord.gg/tF98XvJNvB), under the appropriate channel. While our Discord community often provide helpful advice, email support typically offers the fastest path to resolving your issue.
</Info>

## Enable Error Reporting
<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Configuration">
    Navigate to the configuration page in Superwhisper settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Error Logging">
    Locate and enable the error log toggle switch
    <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/get-started/report-001.png"
    alt="Superwhisper Error Logging"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reporting Through History
The most effective way to report issues related to recordings, is directly through Superwhisper's history.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access History">
    Click the menu icon in Superwhisper and select "History"
    <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/get-started/report-002.png"
    alt="Access Superwhisper History"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Recording">
      Find the problematic recording in the left sidebar and right-click to select "Report Issue"
      <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/images/get-started/report-003.png"
      alt="Report Issue from Superwhisper History"
      />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Collecting Test Recording Data
<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Test Recording">
    Make a brief recording demonstrating the issue
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access History">
    From within the history panel, select the test recording and reveal its folder in Finder
    <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/get-started/report-007.png"
    alt="Reveal Recording in Finder"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compress and Send">
    Compress the selected folder and attach it to your email along with the screenshots
    <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/get-started/report-008.png"
    alt="Compress Recording Files"
    />
  </Step>
