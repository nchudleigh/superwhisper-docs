## Per-Mode Settings

### Voice Processing
After dictating, your audio first goes through voice processing for transcription.

<img
style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
src="/images/modes/modes-002.png"
/>

<Steps>
<Step title="Pick your Model (Cloud or Local)">
Manage all your models in the AI Models tab where you can:
- Use cloud-based models
- Download voice models for local use
- Connect your own API tokens for supported services
</Step>

<Step title="Set your Language">
- Supported languages vary by voice model
- Based on your chosen language and voice model, you may see an option to translate your dictation to English. For best results, use Pro, Standard, or Ultra models
<Note>Your preferred voice model does not support translation to English, or you need translation to another language? Set this up with AI processing by creating a [Custom mode](custom)</Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Control Audio">
Mute & pause recording automatically when dictating with the current mode
</Step>

<Step title="Real-time Processing">
- Real-time transcription is available when using the Nova (Cloud) model
- Toggle this option to see your words appear in the recording window instantly as you speak
</Step>
</Steps>

---
### AI Processing
After transcription, AI processing shapes your text based on the mode's purpose.

<img
style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
src="/images/modes/modes-003.png"
/>

- Access and manage AI models through the dedicated tab
- Built-in modes AI processing support multiple languages automatically
   - Make sure to select your correct language in the [Voice Processing](#voice-processing) settings
   - AI will process your dictation and respond in your chosen language
- AI processing varies by mode
   - Voice Mode does not include AI processing
   - Message, Email, Note, Super, and Meeting modes use optimized AI processing instructions to deliver the best results for their specific purposes
   - Custom Mode lets you take full control by writing your own AI processing instructions
